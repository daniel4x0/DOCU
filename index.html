Chelsea FC Application

Your guide to using the Chelsea FC App - London will always be BLUE.

Installation

Follow these steps to get started with the Chelsea FC App:

  1. Look up "Chelsea FC" and install on your device (refer to compatibility section below).
  2. Click on the "Profile" button on the top right and select "Sign in/Register".
  3. Fill up your details and contact information and click on the email link to verify the email address.

Android Specifications

  • Minimum OS: Android 8.0+
  • RAM: 2GB+
  • Storage: 150MB
  • Processor: Snapdragon 665 or equivalent

iOS Specifications

  • Minimum OS: iOS 12+
  • RAM: 2GB+
  • Storage: 150MB
  • Processor: A10 Fusion or newer

Features

  • Home - Real-time updates of news, transfers and tweets.
  • Watch - Ability to watch matches live and also see highlights of archived games played.
  • Matches - Access previous and future match results and statistics.
  • Explore - Search for keywords, play predictor, tickets and stadium tours.
  • Shop - Buy exclusive Chelsea merch and signed player jerseys

Troubleshooting

Here are some common issues you might encounter:

  • Error 404: Ensure that the URL is correct when installing the app.
  • Live Video Buffering: Close and re-open the video tab and if issue still persists, switch video feed to another one.
  • Connection Issues: Verify that your internet connection is stable when fetching live data.
  • Audio Issues: Ensure that your mobile device is connected to a valid audio output device.
  • Shop Checkout Error: Try again in 30 minutes or access directy from the website.
